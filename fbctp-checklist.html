- fbctf (FaceBook Capture The Flag)-checklist
- Create a capture the flag problem and arange to send it to the ctf game administrators.
- Flags that are embeded in files must be in the format -> flag{the_flag}
      -- Spaces between flag words are NOT permissible.
      -- Use single underscores instead of spaces.
      -- flag{this_is_a_flag} is an acceptable flag
- Flag can not be on localhost computers.  They must be posted on computers that are running 24-7
